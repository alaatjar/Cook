Cook it with potatoes

Potato Cheese Tray Ingredients:
  • 1 ½ potatoes, peeled and cut into circles.
  • 1 piece of onion finely chopped.
  • ¼ cup butter.
  • One cup of milk.
  • 1 cup of cooking cream.
  • 50 grams of grated cheese.
  • Pinch of black pepper.
How to prepare:
  • We boil slices of potatoes for 5 minutes.
  • Then we bring a pot and raise it on the fire and put the butter and then the onions.
  • Put the milk, then the cream with the onions and stir.
  • Prepare a tray and grease it with butter, then pour the milk mixture on it, pour half the amount of cheese over it and stir well.
  • Sprinkle the rest of the cheese over the cheese and sprinkle with black pepper.
  • Then cover well with aluminum foil.
  • Put the tray in the oven and leave for 30 minutes.
